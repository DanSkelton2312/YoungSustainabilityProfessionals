About Us

Young Sustainability Professionals UK is a website established by Dan Skelton in 2023. As a Graduate Sustainability Consultant, Dan believed that sharing his knowledge in the environmental and sustainability sector could help others struggling to get into the field. This website will provide tips on your career search, environmental news, and other things you need to know. We hope you find it useful!

About Me
