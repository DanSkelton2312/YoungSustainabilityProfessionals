About Us

Young Sustainability Professionals UK is a website established by Dan Skelton in 2023. As a Sustainability Professional, Dan believed that sharing his knowledge in the environmental and sustainability sector could help others struggling to get into the field. This website will provide tips on your career search, environmental news, and other things you need to know. We hope you find it useful!

About Me

2017: Began studying Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Biology at the University of Bristol.

2020: The pandemic hits, creating a whole new focus on sustainability.

2020: Began studying Master of Science in Management with Marketing at the University of Bristol, focussing research on corporate sustainability, ESG, and the circular economy.

2022: Started work at Zoomo, a light electric vehicle company aiming to decarbonise transportation.

2022: Joined Mainer Associates as a Sustainability Consultant, working on live sustainability projects.

2023: Is now working for the University of West London as an Environmental Sustainability Officer!
